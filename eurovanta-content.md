## SECTION 2 — HOW IT WORKS

**Section Label:** The Process

**Headline:**
From Application to Employment — A Clear Path

**Subheadline:**
We keep every step simple, transparent, and secure.

---

**Step 1 — Browse & Apply**
Choose a position that matches your skills and country preference. Submit your application through our secure online form. No agents, no middlemen.

**Step 2 — Review & Selection**
Our team reviews every application carefully. Shortlisted candidates are contacted directly for further assessment. All communication comes from official Eurovanta Talent channels only.

**Step 3 — Receive Your Letter**
Successful candidates receive an official Offer Letter or Selection Letter by email. Every letter contains a unique verification code printed clearly on the document.

**Step 4 — Verify & Download**
Go to our Verify section, enter your unique code, and confirm your letter is genuine. Pay the document handling fee to download your official PDF letter instantly.

**Step 5 — Travel & Work**
Follow the onboarding instructions in your letter. Your employer and placement coordinator will guide you through the next steps for legal entry and contract signing.

## 1. Who We Are

Eurovanta Talent ("we," "our," "us") is a recruitment agency connecting job seekers with employers across Europe. For all privacy-related matters, contact us at: privacy@eurovantatalent.com

---

## 2. What Data We Collect

We collect and process the following personal data:

- **Identity Data:** Full name, date of birth, nationality
- **Contact Data:** Email address, phone number, country of residence
- **Professional Data:** CV, employment history, educational qualifications, skills
- **Document Data:** Passport expiry date (at application stage only)
- **Technical Data:** IP address, browser type, device type, pages visited (via cookies)
- **Communication Data:** Records of emails and messages sent to us

---

## 3. Why We Collect Your Data

- To process and assess your job application (contractual necessity)
- To contact you regarding your application status (legitimate interest)
- To verify your identity and eligibility for placement (legal obligation)
- To match your profile with suitable employer vacancies (legitimate interest)
- To comply with EU employment and immigration regulations (legal obligation)
- To improve our website and services (legitimate interest)
- To detect and prevent fraud (legitimate interest and legal obligation)

We will only send marketing communications if you have given explicit consent. You may withdraw that consent at any time.

---

## 4. Lawful Basis for Processing

- **Consent** — for marketing communications and non-essential cookies
- **Contractual necessity** — to process your application and provide our services
- **Legitimate interests** — for fraud prevention, website improvements, and candidate matching
- **Legal obligation** — to comply with applicable EU and national law

---

## 5. How Long We Keep Your Data

| Data Type | Retention Period |
|---|---|
| Unsuccessful applications | 12 months from date of application |
| Successful placements | 5 years from end of employment contract |
| Contact enquiries | 24 months |
| Financial records | 7 years (legal obligation) |
| Cookie data | As defined in Cookie Policy |

After the relevant retention period, data is securely deleted or anonymised.

---

## 6. Who We Share Your Data With

- **Verified employers** — only after selection and with your consent
- **IT service providers** — hosting, email, and database (all under GDPR-compliant data processing agreements)
- **Payment processors** — to process document fees securely (no application data shared)
- **Legal and regulatory authorities** — where required by law

We never sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties.

---

## 7. International Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries within the European Economic Area (EEA). We do not transfer personal data outside the EEA without ensuring adequate safeguards are in place.

---

## 8. Your Rights Under GDPR

- **Right of Access** — request a copy of the data we hold about you
- **Right to Rectification** — ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete data
- **Right to Erasure** — ask us to delete your data where no legal obligation requires retention
- **Right to Restriction** — ask us to limit how we use your data
- **Right to Data Portability** — receive your data in a portable format
- **Right to Object** — object to processing based on legitimate interests
- **Right to Withdraw Consent** — revoke any consent at any time

To exercise any right: privacy@eurovantatalent.com
We will respond within 30 days as required by GDPR.

We use industry-standard encryption, access controls, and secure hosting to protect your personal information. Only authorised personnel have access to application data.

## Our Commitment

Eurovanta Talent is fully committed to compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (EU) 2016/679 ("GDPR"). We respect the privacy and rights of all individuals whose personal data we process.

---

## 1. Data Controller

Eurovanta Talent acts as the Data Controller in respect of all personal data submitted through this website and in the course of our recruitment activities.

Data Protection Contact: privacy@eurovantatalent.com

---

## 2. Data Protection Principles

We comply with all GDPR principles. Personal data is:

- Processed lawfully, fairly, and transparently
- Collected for specified, explicit, and legitimate purposes only
- Adequate, relevant, and limited to what is necessary
- Accurate and kept up to date where necessary
- Kept no longer than necessary for the stated purpose
- Processed securely with appropriate technical and organisational measures

---

## 3. Special Category Data

We do not ask for or process special category data (as defined under GDPR Article 9) — such as health information, ethnic origin, or religious beliefs — in our standard recruitment process.

If any special category data is submitted voluntarily (for example, in a CV), it will be treated with the highest level of protection and not used unless strictly necessary and with explicit consent.

---

## 4. Automated Decision-Making

We do not use automated decision-making or profiling in our selection process. All application reviews and selection decisions involve human judgement by our recruitment team.

---

## 5. Data Breach Procedures

In the event of a personal data breach, we will:

- Notify the relevant supervisory authority within 72 hours of becoming aware, where required by law
- Notify affected individuals without undue delay where the breach is likely to result in a high risk to their rights and freedoms
